Coding Dojo

90 minutes of fun mini-projects, optional show & tell, zero judgment

2-12 Players 90 Minutes Total All Skill Levels

The Goal

Let's spend some time building fun stuff and getting to know each other through code.

Here's the plan: We'll do short 10-15 minute rounds where you build something silly, experimental, or just plain fun. After each round, anyone who wants to can show what they made. No pressure to present - watching is totally fine too.

The rules? There are no rules. Use AI if you want. Try that new framework you've been curious about. Build something ridiculous. Make it pretty, make it ugly, make it weird. This is your playground to experiment without worrying about code reviews or best practices. Let's see what happens when we just have fun with code.

How It Works

1. Code Something (10-15 mins)

Pick an idea and go wild. Quick and dirty? Over-engineered? Your call.

2. Show & Tell (Optional)

Show what you built if you want. Or just enjoy seeing what others made.

3. Rinse & Repeat

We'll do several rounds. Try different challenges, different approaches, different tools.

4. Anything Goes

AI? Sure. New framework? Go for it. Vanilla JS? Classic. Just have fun with it.

Remember: This isn't about perfect code. It's about having fun and maybe learning something new from each other.

How We'll Spend 90 Minutes

We'll do about 5-6 mini rounds over 90 minutes:

  • 10-15 minutes: Code something fun
  • 5 minutes: Optional show & tell (or just watch)
  • Repeat! Try a new challenge or iterate on your last one

Tip: First round is usually the hardest. By round 3, everyone's in the zone.

Ideas for Your 10-15 Minutes

Build anything in 10-15 minutes! The best projects are the ones that let you share a bit about yourself.

Personal & Fun
Something About You

Pet photo gallery? Favorite recipe converter? Hobby tracker? Build something that shows who you are!

Mini Photo Story

Quick slideshow of your weekend, your desk setup, or your coffee collection. Add captions!

Playlist Generator

Generate playlists based on mood, weather, or inside jokes. Share your music taste!

Personal Dashboard

Track something quirky about yourself - coffee count, dad jokes told, bugs squashed.

Quick Code Challenges

Emoji Translator

Convert text to emojis or vice versa. How would you emoji-fy your job title?

Random Generator

Team name generator? Excuse maker? Meeting bingo? Make us laugh!

Quick Game

Speed typing test with team inside jokes? Memory game with coworker faces?

Or Build Whatever You Want!

These are just ideas. The best projects are often the unexpected ones:

  • That tool you've always wanted at work
  • Something related to your hobbies or interests
  • A joke that only your team would understand
  • An experiment with that framework you've been curious about

Remember: Use AI, don't use AI, use any language or tool - whatever helps you build something fun in 15 minutes!
